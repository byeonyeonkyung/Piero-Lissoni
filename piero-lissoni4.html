B&B Italia- Dambo
2023
“Dambo is a modular system that suggests a new manner of seating, a system of large islands. The sofa is easy to put together and features an armrest that moves asymmetrically. I imagined it as an island amid the ebb and flow of everyday life. An island to enjoy alone or in company, listening to music, reading or maybe even browsing on the computer. It is very technological, fully demountable and has been cleaned of any superfluity. We have also added a few surprises in the construction of the cushions, which of course have to remain secret. It has an alluring presence, creating unexpected places that are less casual and more quintessential.”
Piero Lissoni
B&B Italia- Mjna
2021
“Mjna is a very particular chair and reminds me of a manta ray in its litheness. It has a very slender structure and a clean and simple design, but hides within it a special kind of technology… it is a chair that I feel particularly mine.”
Piero Lissoni
